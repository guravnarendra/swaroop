Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and policies

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking appointments in advance to ensure availability. You can call us or use our contact form to schedule your visit.

What safety measures do you follow?

We maintain the highest standards of hygiene and safety. All tools are properly sterilized, and we follow strict cleanliness protocols to ensure a safe environment for all clients.

Do you offer bridal packages?

Yes, we offer comprehensive bridal packages including trial sessions, wedding day makeup, and hair styling. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements and get a customized quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI payments, and all major credit/debit cards. Payment is typically made after the service is completed.
